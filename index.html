About This Project

Artifical meat has been synthesized all over the country and the world through algea, fungi, and micro organisms found on plant roots. But what about the bacteria found on the human body? This project is that next step in that venture. The human body is home to thousands of different types of bacteria, and one of them could be the key to synthesizing the most delicious burger you've ever had. However, with so many types of bacteria all over the human body, where do we start? We decided the belly button. This project looks at the bacteria present in the belly buttons of over a hundred volunteers in hopes of discovering a bacteria to aid in that goal.

Here we have visualizations of the results from the volunteers. We have the 10 ten bacteria cultures found in each volunteer, a representation of all the bacteria cultures found for each volunteer, and a guage informing how often the volunteer washes their belly button per week if the information was provided.
